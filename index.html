Wanna Save the World?

Saving the environment doesn’t require crazy changes, small actions can have a big impact! You can start by making simple yet helpful choices like recycling plastic, turning off lights when not in use, or picking up litter near your area. Use public transportation, biking, or walking instead of driving whenever convenient to reduce harmful gasses. Even spreading awareness can help greatly when it comes to the environment. With Eco Life, you can post good habits, compete with others in the leaderboard, and be part of a community that's dedicated to helping our planet. Every effort counts so why not start helping today?

What is Eco Life?

Eco Life is a web application that motivates people to change their lifestyle and be environmentally friendly. Our platform provides information about environmental matters to the users and challenges them to contribute to the cause in their day to day activities. Any activity that is eco-friendly for instance recycling, saving energy or even minimizing on waste then the user can enter and describe the action taken, post it to the community and even get points for being active on the leaderboard. With a simple and intuitive design, and interesting elements, Eco Life makes the process of sustainability both easy and enjoyable. Let us change the world together – one action at a time!
